# BA_Furniture / Noi That Phu Vuong - Showroom Functional Architecture Brief

## 1. Muc tieu

Ho so nay dat lai thu tu thiet ke cho showroom BA_Furniture / Noi That Phu Vuong theo nguyen tac: **cong nang va van hanh di truoc, ket cau kiem tra theo sau**.

Ban ve ket cau hien co chi dung de doi chieu o buoc sau. Khong lay dam, cot, nhip ket cau hien tai de ep mat bang kien truc khi chua chot cong nang.

## 2. Du lieu dau vao dang su dung

- Loai cong trinh: showroom noi that ket hop kho, van phong, trung bay, ban hang va marketing.
- Thuong hieu: BA_Furniture / Noi That Phu Vuong.
- Quy mo tham chieu: ngang khoang 10m, sau khoang 18m.
- Co phan dua mat tien / console khoang 1.4m.
- Co phan sau dung cho ban cong, ky thuat hoac thoang sau khoang 1.5m.
- Mai che / mai kinh mat tien tham chieu khoang 2m.
- Tang 1 uu tien kho va nhap xuat hang.
- Tang 2 uu tien van phong, tiep khach, tu van, bao gia.
- Tang 3 uu tien showroom noi that van phong.
- Tang 4 uu tien showroom noi that gia dinh / home.
- Tang 5 uu tien phu tro, sinh hoat, studio chup anh / livestream, kho nhe.
- Mat sau tang 1 khong phai cong nhap hang chinh.
- Hang tiep can chu yeu tu mat tien tang 1.
- Thang may / thang hang la tuyen van chuyen chinh len cac tang.
- Mat tien can sang, hien dai, kinh lon, phang, co tinh thuong mai, bam tinh than Govi.vn nhung khong sao chep.

## 3. Nguyen tac thiet ke

1. Khach hang phai thay thuong hieu va loi vao ro rang truoc.
2. Tuyen khach khong di xuyen qua khu kho lon.
3. Tuyen hang di ngan, thang, de boc do, khong cat qua khu trung bay dep nhat.
4. Showroom phai co cau truc nhom san pham ro, khong xep hang nhu kho.
5. Tang nao phuc vu van hanh thi dung vai tro van hanh; tang nao phuc vu ban hang thi dung vai tro ban hang.
6. Mat tien uu tien nhan dien, anh sang, kinh lon va trai nghiem nhin tu duong.
7. Ket cau chi kiem tra sau khi cong nang va kien truc da hop ly.

## 4. Nhom nguoi dung va nhu cau

### 4.1 Khach le
- Can nhin thay showroom ro tu mat duong.
- Can loi vao de hieu, khong bi lan voi cong nhap hang.
- Can di chuyen len cac tang showroom de trai nghiem san pham.
- Can diem tu van, bao gia, chot don.

### 4.2 Khach du an / doanh nghiep
- Can tiep khach trang trong hon khach le.
- Can phong hop hoac ban trao doi rieng.
- Can catalogue, mau vat lieu, QR san pham, combo theo phong ban.
- Can cam giac cong ty co nang luc cung cap du an.

### 4.3 Nhan vien
- Can khu lam viec rieng, khong bi khach va hang gay nhieu.
- Can khu luu ho so, may in, bao gia, don hang.
- Can di chuyen giua van phong, showroom va kho.

### 4.4 Giao hang / nhap hang
- Can cua cuon lon phia truoc.
- Can khu tam dung, kiem hang, dong goi.
- Can tiep can thang hang nhanh.
- Khong nen di qua khu tiep khach hoac showroom dep.

### 4.5 Marketing / content
- Can khu chup anh, quay video, livestream, tao noi dung san pham.
- Can nen sach, anh sang on dinh, de thay doi background.
- Tang 5 la vi tri phu hop nhat neu khong anh huong ban hang truc tiep.

## 5. Phan tang cong nang de xuat

### Tang 1 - Kho nhap xuat va logistics
Vai tro: van hanh hang hoa.

Thanh phan:
- Cua cuon / cong nhap hang phia truoc.
- Khu tam dung hang.
- Khu kiem hang / dan ma / dong goi.
- Kho chinh.
- Loi nguoi di rieng nho gon.
- Thang may / thang hang gan tuyen kho.
- Mot diem tiep don nho neu can, nhung khong bien tang 1 thanh showroom chinh.

### Tang 2 - Van phong, tiep khach, tu van
Vai tro: dau nao van hanh va lam viec voi khach du an.

Thanh phan:
- Khu tiep khach / reception nho.
- Van phong lam viec.
- Ban tu van / bao gia.
- Phong hop nho.
- Khu catalogue, mau vat lieu, QR san pham.
- Kho ho so / may in / vat tu van phong.

### Tang 3 - Showroom noi that van phong
Vai tro: tang ban hang chinh cho nhom office.

Nhom trung bay:
- Ghe lanh dao.
- Ghe nhan vien.
- Ghe phong hop.
- Ban lam viec.
- Ban giam doc.
- Tu tai lieu.
- Hoc, tu phu.
- Combo phong lam viec mau.
- Khu trai nghiem ghe.
- Diem tu van va chot don.

### Tang 4 - Showroom home / living
Vai tro: tang tao cam xuc va hinh anh thuong hieu cao cap hon.

Nhom trung bay:
- Sofa.
- Ban tra.
- Ban an.
- Tu / ke trang tri.
- Phong khach mau.
- Phong an mau.
- Goc san pham gia tot.

### Tang 5 - Phu tro, studio, sinh hoat
Vai tro: ho tro van hanh va marketing.

Thanh phan:
- Studio chup anh / livestream.
- Kho nhe.
- Khu an nhe / nghi nhan vien.
- Phong lam viec marketing / content.
- Ky thuat / dieu hoa / thiet bi phu tro.

## 6. Tuyen van hanh

### 6.1 Tuyen khach
Mat tien -> sanh / loi vao -> thang / thang may -> tang 2 tiep khach hoac tang 3-4 showroom -> diem tu van -> quay lai thang.

### 6.2 Tuyen hang
Cua cuon tang 1 -> tam dung -> kiem hang -> kho -> thang hang -> tang 3/4 trung bay hoac tang 5 kho nhe.

### 6.3 Tuyen nhan vien
Loi vao rieng hoac dung chung sanh -> tang 2 van phong -> di chuyen len/xuong showroom/kho theo nhu cau.

## 7. Nguyen tac mat tien

- Kinh lon tu tang 2 len tang 5.
- It chia o nho; uu tien mang phang, sach, hien dai.
- Tang 1 chac chan, gon, dung cho kho va loi vao.
- Bien hieu ro: BA_Furniture va/hoac NOI THAT PHU VUONG.
- Co he den nhan dien ban dem.
- Mau nen de xuat: trang, ghi sang, den nhom, kinh xanh nhe, diem nhan vang/dong neu dung logo Phu Vuong.
- Khong dung trang tri gia co dien, cot gia, phao chi nang ne.

## 8. Cac dieu khong duoc lam

- Khong lay ket cau hien co de ep cong nang khi chua chot mat bang.
- Khong de khach phai di xuyen kho moi len showroom.
- Khong de thang bo hoac thang hang pha mat tien chinh.
- Khong tron showroom office va home qua roi tren cung mot tang neu khong can thiet.
- Khong bien tang 1 thanh showroom dep nhung lam hong logistics.
- Khong chia mat tien qua nhieu o nho lam mat tinh thuong mai.
- Khong bo qua khu tu van/chot don.
- Khong bo qua nhu cau chup anh/livestream san pham.

## 9. Viec can doi chieu ket cau sau

Sau khi chot zoning, can kiem tra:
- Vi tri cot co pha loi vao, cua cuon, showroom khong.
- Vi tri dam co anh huong tran, chieu cao thong thuy, mat dung kinh khong.
- Vi tri thang may / thang hang co hop ly voi tuyen hang khong.
- Tai trong san tang 3-4 co phu hop showroom noi that khong.
- Khu kho tang 1 va kho nhe tang 5 co can tang cuong tai trong khong.
- Mat tien kinh va console 1.4m co can dieu chinh theo ket cau khong.

## 10. Open questions

- Kich thuoc chinh xac tung tang sau khi tru dien tich thang, WC, ho ky thuat.
- Vi tri thang may/thang hang chot cuoi.
- Co can tach thang khach va thang hang hay dung chung.
- So luong nhan vien lam viec thuong xuyen tai tang 2.
- Muc tieu showroom: uu tien office hay home hon.
- Bien hieu chinh dung BA_Furniture, Noi That Phu Vuong, hay ca hai.
